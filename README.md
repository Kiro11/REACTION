What is this project and what does it do?
This is the last project I made, and it's a fun little one. It's basically a website you go to and it tests your reaction time — it shows you your best score ever and the score you got this attempt, and over time it helps you actually get faster. Simple, but kind of addictive.

How was it built?
HTML, CSS, JS.

Honestly, most of it was me. Roughly 95% HTML, 85% CSS, 90% JS was written by hand. The percentage where AI helped is pretty small — the styling wasn't really that hard, so AI only helped with a tiny bit of it, plus the font used for the word "React." For the JavaScript, it helped me understand a few pieces of logic, nothing more than that.
